## Customer Journey

### Phase 1: Discovery & Registration

<div class="feature-box">
<h4>1. Download & Onboarding</h4>
<p><strong>Scenario:</strong> Sarah hears about Re-USED from her municipality newsletter</p>
<ul>
  <li>Downloads app from App Store or Google Play</li>
  <li>Quick 3-screen onboarding explaining the concept</li>
  <li>Links her municipal ID card or creates account with phone number</li>
  <li><strong>Time:</strong> 2 minutes</li>
</ul>
</div>

### Phase 2: First Use

<div class="feature-box">
<h4>2. Finding a Location</h4>
<p><strong>Scenario:</strong> Sarah has old batteries at home and wants to recycle them</p>
<ul>
  <li>Opens app and taps "Find Location" button</li>
  <li>Map shows 3 collection points within 500m</li>
  <li>Selects nearest point, gets walking directions</li>
  <li>Sees "tap your phone to unlock" instruction</li>
  <li><strong>Time:</strong> 30 seconds</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="feature-box">
<h4>3. First Deposit</h4>
<p><strong>Scenario:</strong> Sarah arrives at the container with her batteries</p>
<ul>
  <li>Holds phone near NFC reader on container</li>
  <li>Container unlocks with green light and sound</li>
  <li>Deposits 8 AA batteries and 2 phone batteries</li>
  <li>Container automatically closes</li>
  <li>App shows: "+10 points earned! ğŸ‰"</li>
  <li><strong>Time:</strong> 45 seconds</li>
</ul>
</div>

### Phase 3: Engagement & Habit Formation

<div class="feature-box">
<h4>4. Impact Notification</h4>
<p><strong>Scenario:</strong> 1 hour after first deposit</p>
<ul>
  <li>Push notification: "Great start! You saved 50g of CO2 emissions"</li>
  <li>Opens app to see personalized impact dashboard</li>
  <li>Discovers she's in top 30% of new users</li>
  <li>Shares achievement badge on social media</li>
  <li><strong>Time:</strong> 2 minutes</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="feature-box">
<h4>5. Establishing Routine</h4>
<p><strong>Scenario:</strong> Over the next 3 months</p>
<ul>
  <li>Receives location-based reminder when passing collection point</li>
  <li>Joins neighborhood recycling challenge (50 participants)</li>
  <li>Deposits batteries 6 more times</li>
  <li>Accumulates 75 points total</li>

### Phase 4: Advocacy & Retention

## Technical Stack (Planned)

- **Frontend:** React Native (iOS & Android)
- **Backend:** Node.js + PostgreSQL
- **Maps:** OpenStreetMap / Google Maps API
- **NFC:** Native mobile NFC APIs
- **Authentication:** OAuth 2.0 + Municipal ID integration
- **Push Notifications:** Firebase Cloud Messaging
- **Analytics:** Privacy-focused event tracking
